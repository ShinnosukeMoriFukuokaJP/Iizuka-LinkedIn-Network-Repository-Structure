# Roadmap

**Iizuka LinkedIn Network** · Chikuho Region · Fukuoka Prefecture · Japan

This roadmap outlines our path from launch to becoming Japan's leading regional innovation community.

---

## Phase 1: Seed (Q1 2025)

**Goal: Launch and establish foundation**

- [x] Repository created and structured
- [x] README.md, CONTRIBUTING.md, and core documentation published
- [x] LinkedIn Group created
- [ ] First 10 members onboarded
- [ ] Core resource pages published (AI_RESOURCES.md, DX_RESOURCES.md)
- [ ] First networking event in Iizuka City
- [ ] Newsletter infrastructure set up

---

## Phase 2: Growth (Q2–Q3 2025)

**Goal: 100 members · Community activation**

- [ ] 100 active LinkedIn Group members
- [ ] 50+ curated resources published
- [ ] Monthly event cadence established
- [ ] First 3 corporate or institutional partners
- [ ] Media coverage in Fukuoka Prefecture local press
- [ ] 10+ community-contributed articles
- [ ] GitHub repository starred 50+ times

---

## Phase 3: Momentum (Q4 2025)

**Goal: 500 members · Regional recognition**

- [ ] 500 active members across Iizuka City, Tagawa City, Nogata City
- [ ] Recognized by Fukuoka Prefecture business organizations
- [ ] Sponsor partnerships established
- [ ] First annual Chikuho Innovation Summit
- [ ] 100+ curated resources
- [ ] Member success stories documented (5+ cases)
- [ ] SEO rankings achieved for target keywords

---

## Phase 4: Scale (2026)

**Goal: 1,000+ members · National footprint**

- [ ] 1,000+ active members
- [ ] National media coverage
- [ ] Partnership with Fukuoka Prefecture government initiatives
- [ ] University partnerships (Fukuoka Prefecture institutions)
- [ ] Launch of formal mentorship program
- [ ] Regional AI and DX certification pathway
- [ ] Sister community partnerships in other Japanese prefectures

---

## Phase 5: Expand (2027 and beyond)

**Goal: National model · International visibility**

- [ ] 10,000+ members across Japan
- [ ] Replicable community model published for other regions
- [ ] International knowledge exchange partnerships
- [ ] Contribution to Japan's national Regional Revitalization strategy
- [ ] Global recognition as a model for regional innovation communities

---

## Ongoing Priorities

- Maintain free and open access for all members
- Ensure content quality and relevance to the Chikuho Region
- Continuously improve SEO discoverability
- Support contributor recognition and growth
- Expand language accessibility (Japanese and English)
