# Roadmap

**Iizuka LinkedIn Network** · Chikuho Region · Fukuoka Prefecture · Japan

Our path from a GitHub repository to Japan's most impactful regional professional ecosystem platform.

---

## Vision Statement

By 2030, **Iizuka LinkedIn Network** will be recognized as the national model for community-driven regional revitalization — demonstrating that any region in Japan can build a thriving professional, AI, and startup ecosystem from the ground up, powered by open knowledge and genuine community.

## How to Contribute to the Roadmap

Have ideas for milestones, priorities, or new directions?

1. Open a GitHub Issue with label `roadmap`
2. Start a discussion in the LinkedIn Group
3. Email [community@iizuka-linkedin-network.org](mailto:community@iizuka-linkedin-network.org)

All community members can propose roadmap changes. Major shifts go through community discussion.

---

## Roadmap Principles

- **Community first**: Milestones serve the Chikuho Region, not metrics for their own sake
- **Open always**: Core resources remain free forever
- **Honest progress**: We track what's done and what isn't without overstating
- **Adaptable**: The roadmap evolves with the community's needs
