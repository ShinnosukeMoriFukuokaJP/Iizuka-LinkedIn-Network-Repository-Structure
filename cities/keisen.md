## Overview

**Keisen Town** (桂川町) is located in the **Chikuho Region** of **Fukuoka Prefecture**, Japan. Keisen Town sits at the geographic center of Fukuoka Prefecture, making it a strategic location for logistics and residential development. The town offers a quiet, community-oriented lifestyle with growing commuter appeal.
