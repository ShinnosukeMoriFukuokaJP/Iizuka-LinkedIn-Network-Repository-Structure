## Overview

**Fukuchi Town** (福智町) is located in the **Chikuho Region** of **Fukuoka Prefecture**, Japan. Fukuchi Town is a well-rounded community with a mix of manufacturing, agriculture, and retail. Its location between Iizuka and Tagawa makes it a natural hub for cross-regional commerce and community initiatives.

| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Population** | ~24,000 |
| **Prefecture** | Fukuoka |

## Startup Opportunities

Fukuchi Town presents unique startup opportunities for founders willing to build outside major urban centers:

- **Untapped local market** for digital services, AI consulting, and DX support
