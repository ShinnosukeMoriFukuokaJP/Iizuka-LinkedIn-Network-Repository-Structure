## Overview

**Iizuka City** (飯塚市) is located in the **Chikuho Region** of **Fukuoka Prefecture**, Japan. The largest city in the Chikuho Region and home to Kyushu Institute of Technology, Iizuka City is the economic and educational center of the region. Historically a coal mining hub, Iizuka has reinvented itself as a center for manufacturing, healthcare, and increasingly, digital innovation.
