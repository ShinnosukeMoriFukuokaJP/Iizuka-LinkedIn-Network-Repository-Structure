## Overview

**Kama City** (嘉麻市) is located in the **Chikuho Region** of **Fukuoka Prefecture**, Japan. Kama City was formed in 2006 through the merger of four towns. Surrounded by mountains and forests, Kama has strong agricultural roots and is exploring agritech and eco-tourism as growth vectors.

| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Population** | ~35,000 |
| **Prefecture** | Fukuoka |
| **Region** | Chikuho |
| **Key Industries** | Agriculture, Manufacturing, Forestry, Healthcare |

## AI Opportunities

**Artificial Intelligence** adoption in Kama City is at an early stage — creating first-mover advantages:
