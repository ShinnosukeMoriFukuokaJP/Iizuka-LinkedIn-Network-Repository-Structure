## Overview

**Kotake Town** (小竹町) is located in the **Chikuho Region** of **Fukuoka Prefecture**, Japan. One of the smaller municipalities in the Chikuho Region, Kotake Town maintains a strong community identity with roots in the coal mining era. Its compact size makes it an ideal environment for tight-knit community innovation projects.
