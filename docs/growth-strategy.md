# Growth Strategy

**Iizuka LinkedIn Network** · Chikuho Region · Fukuoka Prefecture · Japan

---

## First 10 Members

**Timeline**: Week 1–2
**Channel**: Direct, high-touch outreach

1. Founder posts announcement on personal LinkedIn with repository link
2. Direct message to 10 hand-picked professionals in Iizuka City
3. Reach out to Iizuka Chamber of Commerce (飯塚商工会議所)
4. Post in Fukuoka-related LinkedIn Groups
5. Email Kyushu Institute of Technology career office
6. Share in any existing Chikuho region LINE or Slack groups
7. Post in relevant Japanese Twitter/X communities (#福岡 #筑豊 #LinkedIn)
8. GitHub repository starred by founder's personal network
9. Host a 30-minute online introduction call
10. Follow up personally with everyone who engages

**Goal**: 10 members who are genuinely interested and will invite others.

---

## First 100 Members

**Timeline**: Month 1–3
**Channel**: Content marketing + events

1. **LinkedIn content series** (3 posts/week for 12 weeks):
   - Week 1–4: "Why I built this" storytelling
   - Week 5–8: City spotlights (Iizuka, Tagawa, Nogata)
   - Week 9–12: AI and DX insights for Chikuho businesses
2. **First in-person event** in Iizuka City (target: 20–30 attendees)
3. **Partner with 3 local organizations**: Chamber of Commerce, university, business association
4. **Guest post on Fukuoka business media**
5. **Referral ask**: Every new member invited to bring 1 person
6. **GitHub README SEO**: Optimize for target keywords
7. **Newsletter launch**: Monthly, distributed on LinkedIn and email

**Goal**: 100 engaged members, 1 event completed, 3 partners signed.

## First 1,000 Members

**Timeline**: Month 4–12
**Channel**: Community flywheel + media

1. **Monthly event cadence**: Rotating across Iizuka, Tagawa, Nogata
2. **Chikuho Innovation Summit**: Annual flagship event (target: 200 attendees)
3. **Media coverage**: Nishinippon Shimbun, NHK Fukuoka, regional business press
4. **Corporate partnerships**: 5 companies promote membership to employees
5. **University partnership**: Kyushu Institute of Technology student program
6. **Content volume**: 100+ articles, resources, and city pages published
7. **SEO rankings**: Achieve top 3 for `Iizuka LinkedIn`, `Chikuho AI`
8. **LinkedIn Group engagement**: Daily posts, weekly Q&As, monthly AMAs
9. **Newsletter growth**: 500+ subscribers with 40%+ open rate
10. **Success stories**: 10+ documented member wins published

**Goal**: 1,000 members, monthly events, national media mention, sustainable growth flywheel.

## LinkedIn Integration Strategy

### LinkedIn Group
- Primary community hub for discussion and networking
- Post 5x/week: 2 resources, 2 discussions, 1 spotlight
- Weekly Q&A post with open question for members
- Monthly "New Member Monday" welcome post

### LinkedIn Newsletter
- Monthly newsletter: "Chikuho Region Professional Digest"
- Sections: AI news, DX case study, job spotlight, event preview, member story
- Target: 1,000 subscribers by Month 6

### LinkedIn Events
- All in-person events also listed as LinkedIn Events
- Online-only events for national reach
- Use LinkedIn Events for RSVP and reminder automation

### Cross-Platform Growth
```
LinkedIn Post → "Full article in GitHub repo →" link
GitHub README → "Join LinkedIn Group" CTA
Newsletter → GitHub and LinkedIn Group links
Events → Mention both GitHub resources and LinkedIn Group
```
