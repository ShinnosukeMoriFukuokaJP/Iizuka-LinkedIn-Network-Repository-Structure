# Research

**Iizuka LinkedIn Network** · Chikuho Region · Fukuoka Prefecture · Japan

Academic research, data, and reports relevant to the **Chikuho Region**'s economic and social transformation.

---

## Research Themes

- Regional revitalization (地方創生) in post-industrial Japan
- AI and DX adoption in rural and regional SMEs
- Professional networking in non-metropolitan Japan
- Labor market dynamics in Fukuoka Prefecture
- Innovation ecosystems in secondary cities
