## Recurring Event Series

### 🤝 Monthly Networking Meetup
**Format**: In-person · Rotating across Iizuka City, Tagawa City, Nogata City  
**Frequency**: Monthly (first Thursday)  
**Who**: Professionals of all backgrounds  
**Cost**: Free  
Casual, relaxed networking. Bring your business card and your questions.

---

### 🤖 AI Workshop Series
**Format**: Hybrid (in-person + Zoom)  
**Frequency**: Bi-monthly (every 2 months)  
**Who**: Business owners, professionals new to AI  
**Cost**: Free  
Hands-on sessions with real tools: ChatGPT, Claude, n8n, and more. No technical background required.

---

### 💻 DX Seminar for SMEs
**Format**: In-person · Iizuka City  
**Frequency**: Quarterly  
**Who**: Business owners, operations managers  
**Cost**: Free  
Deep-dive Digital Transformation workshops with case studies from actual Chikuho Region businesses.

---

### 🚀 Startup Pitch Night
**Format**: In-person · Iizuka City  
**Frequency**: Quarterly  
**Who**: Founders, investors, potential co-founders, supporters  
**Cost**: Free  
Present your idea or product. Get feedback from peers, mentors, and potential customers. All stages welcome.

---

### 💼 LinkedIn Strategy Workshop
**Format**: Online (Zoom)  
**Frequency**: Monthly  
**Who**: Professionals looking to improve LinkedIn visibility and networking  
**Cost**: Free  
Profile reviews, content strategy, and live Q&A on LinkedIn for Japanese market professionals.

---

### 🎯 Career Development Roundtable
**Format**: Hybrid  
**Frequency**: Bi-monthly  
**Who**: Job seekers, career changers, professionals seeking growth  
**Cost**: Free  
Peer support, job search strategy, interview preparation, and AI tools for career development.

## Annual Events

### 🏆 Chikuho Innovation Summit
**Format**: In-person · Iizuka City  
**Frequency**: Annual (target: October/November)  
**Who**: Regional and national professionals, entrepreneurs, academics, government  
**Cost**: Free (or minimal donation)  

The flagship annual event bringing together the best of the Chikuho Region's innovation ecosystem. Features keynotes, panels, startup pitches, workshops, and networking.

**Target attendance**: 100 (Year 1) → 300 (Year 3) → 500+ (Year 5)
