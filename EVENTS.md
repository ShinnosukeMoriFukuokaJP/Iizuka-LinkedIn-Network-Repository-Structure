## Recurring Events

### Monthly Networking Meetup
**Format**: In-person · Rotating venues across Iizuka City, Tagawa City, Nogata City
**Frequency**: Monthly
**Description**: Informal professional networking for members of all backgrounds. Bring your business cards and your ideas.

### AI Workshop Series
**Format**: Hybrid (in-person + online)
**Frequency**: Bi-monthly
**Description**: Hands-on sessions covering practical AI and Generative AI tools for Chikuho Region businesses and professionals.

### DX Seminar
**Format**: In-person
**Frequency**: Quarterly
**Description**: Deep-dive Digital Transformation workshops tailored for SMEs in Fukuoka Prefecture.

### Startup Pitch Night
**Format**: In-person · Iizuka City
**Frequency**: Quarterly
**Description**: Local entrepreneurs pitch their ideas to peers, mentors, and potential investors. Open to all stages.

### LinkedIn Strategy Workshop
**Format**: Online
**Frequency**: Monthly
**Description**: Practical sessions on LinkedIn profile optimization, content strategy, and networking for Fukuoka Prefecture professionals.

### Career Development Roundtable
**Format**: Hybrid
**Frequency**: Bi-monthly
**Description**: Peer support for career changers, job seekers, and professionals looking to grow in the Chikuho Region.

## Annual Events

### Chikuho Innovation Summit
**Format**: In-person · Iizuka City
**Frequency**: Annual (target: Q4)
**Description**: The flagship annual event bringing together professionals, entrepreneurs, AI practitioners, and community leaders from across Fukuoka Prefecture and beyond.
