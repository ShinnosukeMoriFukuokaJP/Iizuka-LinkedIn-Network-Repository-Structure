## Regional Revitalization

The **地方創生 (Regional Revitalization)** policy framework provides significant funding and support for communities like the Chikuho Region. Key programs:

- **デジタル田園都市国家構想 (Digital Garden City Nation)** — Digital infrastructure for rural and regional Japan
- **地方創生交付金 (Regional Revitalization Grants)** — Funding for local innovation projects
- **関係人口 (Kankei Jinko)** — Programs to connect urban professionals with regional communities
