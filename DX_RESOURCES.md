## What is Digital Transformation?

Digital Transformation (DX) is the process of integrating digital technology into all areas of a business, fundamentally changing how you operate and deliver value to customers. For **Chikuho Region** SMEs, DX is not about becoming a tech company — it is about using technology to work smarter, reduce costs, and serve customers better.

## DX Implementation Roadmap for SMEs

### Stage 1: Digital Foundation (Months 1–3)
- Migrate from paper to digital document management
- Set up cloud storage (Google Drive or OneDrive)
- Establish team communication channels (Slack or Teams)
- Set up basic CRM for customer management

### Stage 2: Process Automation (Months 4–6)
- Automate repetitive tasks using Zapier or n8n
- Digitize invoicing and accounting
- Set up automated email workflows
- Create a company knowledge base

### Stage 3: Data-Driven Operations (Months 7–12)
- Implement dashboards for key business metrics
- Use data to inform marketing and sales decisions
- Begin AI integration into core workflows
- Train team on digital tools and best practices
