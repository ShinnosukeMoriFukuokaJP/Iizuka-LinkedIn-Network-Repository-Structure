## What Is Artificial Intelligence?

**In plain Japanese business terms:**  
AIは、人間が行う判断・文章作成・分析・翻訳などの作業を、コンピューターが自動的に行う技術です。

**In plain English:**  
AI is software that can do tasks that used to require human intelligence — like writing, analyzing, translating, answering questions, and generating images.

**For Chikuho Region business owners:**  
AI is a tool that can save you time on repetitive tasks, help you write better content, answer customer questions, and analyze your business data — without needing to write code.

## Your First 3 AI Experiments (Try Today)

### Experiment 1: Rewrite an email
Copy a business email you wrote this week. Ask Claude:
```
Please rewrite this email to be more professional and concise. 
Keep the key message but make it clearer:

[paste your email]
```

### Experiment 2: Summarize a document
Paste a long document (article, report, contract) and ask:
```
Please summarize this in 5 bullet points in Japanese:

[paste document]
```

### Experiment 3: Get a business idea
Tell Claude about your business and ask:
```
I run a [type of business] in Iizuka City, Fukuoka Prefecture. 
It has [number] employees and mainly serves [type of customer].
What are 5 ways I could use AI to save time or grow the business?
```

## Prompt Engineering Basics

A **prompt** is what you type to the AI. Better prompts = better results.

### The 4-Part Prompt Formula
```
ROLE: "You are a [role]..."
CONTEXT: "My business is [description]..."
TASK: "Please [what you want]..."
FORMAT: "Output as [bullet points / table / email / etc.]"
```

**Bad prompt**: "Write a LinkedIn post"

**Good prompt**: 
```
You are a professional LinkedIn content writer. 
My business is an AI consulting firm in Iizuka City, 
Fukuoka Prefecture, Japan serving local SMEs. 
Please write a LinkedIn post about how small businesses 
in regional Japan can benefit from AI tools. 
Output as a 150-word post with 3 hashtags.
```

## Common Beginner Mistakes

| Mistake | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| Prompt too vague | Add context: who you are, what you need, in what format |
| Accepting first output | Ask AI to revise: "Make it shorter / more formal / in Japanese" |
| Not fact-checking | AI can make things up. Always verify important facts |
| Sharing confidential data | Never paste private customer data or trade secrets into free AI tools |
| Giving up after one try | Reprompt with feedback: "That's too long. Try again in 100 words." |
