## Why Prompting Matters

The same AI tool can produce mediocre results or exceptional results — the difference is how you write your prompt. **Prompt engineering** is the skill of writing instructions that reliably get you what you want.

For **Chikuho Region professionals**, this means:
- Getting AI to write in natural Japanese
- Getting business-relevant, not generic, outputs
- Saving time instead of spending time fixing AI outputs

## The CRAFT Framework

| Letter | Element | Example |
|--------|---------|---------|
| **C** | Context | "I am a manufacturing SME owner in Iizuka City..." |
| **R** | Role | "You are an expert Japanese business writer..." |
| **A** | Action | "Write a LinkedIn post about..." |
| **F** | Format | "Output as a 150-word post with 3 hashtags..." |
| **T** | Tone | "Professional but approachable, Japanese business style" |

## Prompt Templates for Japanese Business

### LinkedIn Post
```
You are a Japanese business LinkedIn content expert.
I am [your role] at [your company/type] in [your city], Fukuoka Prefecture.
Write a LinkedIn post about [topic].
- Length: 150–200 words
- Tone: Professional but human
- Include: 1 specific insight from my context
- End with: 1 engaging question for readers
- Add: 3–4 relevant hashtags including #筑豊 or #福岡
Output in Japanese.
```

### Business Email (Japanese)
```
Write a professional Japanese business email.
From: [your role]
To: [recipient role] at [their company type]
Purpose: [what you want to achieve]
Key points to include:
- [point 1]
- [point 2]
Tone: Formal Japanese business style (敬語)
Length: Concise, under 200 characters in body
```

### Meeting Summary
```
Here are my meeting notes:
[paste raw notes]

Please organize these into:
1. Key decisions made (箇条書き)
2. Action items with owner and deadline
3. Next meeting agenda items
Output in Japanese, professional format.
```

### Customer FAQ Response
```
You are a customer service representative for [type of business] 
in Fukuoka Prefecture, Japan.
A customer asked: "[customer question]"
Write a helpful, friendly response in Japanese.
- Answer completely but concisely
- Offer next steps if relevant
- Sign off professionally
```

## Advanced Techniques

### Chain of Thought
Ask AI to show its reasoning:
```
Think step by step about [problem].
First, consider [aspect 1].
Then analyze [aspect 2].
Finally, recommend [outcome].
```

### Few-Shot Examples
Show AI what you want:
```
Write 3 LinkedIn post titles in this style:

Example 1: "3 AI tools that saved my Iizuka City business 10 hours/week"
Example 2: "Why I stopped cold-calling and started posting on LinkedIn"
Example 3: "The DX mistake that cost a Fukuoka SME 6 months"

Now write 5 more in the same style about [your topic].
```

### Iterative Refinement
```
First draft: [your prompt]
→ AI output →
"Good start. Now make it: shorter / more formal / more specific to 
Chikuho Region / in Japanese / with a stronger call to action"
```

## Common Japanese Business Prompting Tips

- Always specify **敬語 level**: 丁寧語 (polite), 尊敬語 (respectful), or casual
- Ask for **読点の調整** (punctuation adjustment) for natural flow
- Specify **文体**: ですます調 (formal) or だ・である調 (assertive)
- For LinkedIn: Japanese audiences prefer slightly longer posts than Western counterparts
