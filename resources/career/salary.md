## Cost of Living Advantage

Chikuho Region professionals benefit from significantly lower living costs:

| Expense | Tokyo | Fukuoka City | Iizuka / Chikuho |
|---------|-------|-------------|------------------|
| 1LDK rent (central) | ¥150,000+/mo | ¥70,000–90,000/mo | ¥40,000–60,000/mo |
| Monthly food budget | ¥60,000+ | ¥40,000–50,000 | ¥30,000–40,000 |
| Effective purchasing power | Baseline | ~130% of Tokyo | ~160–180% of Tokyo |

> A ¥5M salary in Iizuka City provides comparable or higher quality of life than ¥7–8M in Tokyo.
