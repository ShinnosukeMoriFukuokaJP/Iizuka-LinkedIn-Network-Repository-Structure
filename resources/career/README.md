- [ ] 2,000+ character About section
- [ ] Featured section with your best work
- [ ] 500+ connections
- [ ] Regular content posting (3x/week minimum)

## Job Search Strategy for Chikuho Professionals

1. **Optimize LinkedIn** for Fukuoka Prefecture keywords
2. **Connect with local decision-makers** in your target companies
3. **Post content** demonstrating your expertise
4. **Join the ILN LinkedIn Group** for local referrals
5. **Attend local events** to meet hiring managers in person
6. **Use AI tools** (Claude, ChatGPT) to optimize your resume
