## Business Registration in Japan

### Business Structures

| Structure | JP | Best For | Cost |
|-----------|-----|---------|------|
| Sole Proprietor | 個人事業主 | Freelancers, early testing | ~¥0 |
| LLC (GK) | 合同会社 | Small teams, cost-conscious | ~¥60,000 |
| Corporation (KK) | 株式会社 | Fundraising, credibility | ~¥200,000 |

### Registering as Individual Business Owner (個人事業主)
1. File **開業届** at your local tax office (税務署) — Free
2. Consider filing **青色申告承認申請書** for tax benefits
3. Register for consumption tax if revenue > ¥10,000,000

### Registering a Corporation (KK/GK)
1. Draft articles of incorporation (定款)
2. Notarize at a notary office (公証役場) — Required for KK
3. Register at Legal Affairs Bureau (法務局)
4. Open corporate bank account
5. File with tax office and social insurance

## Tax Obligations

- **Consumption Tax (消費税)**: 10% standard rate
- **Corporate Tax (法人税)**: ~23.2% on profits
- **Individual Income Tax**: Progressive 5–45%
- **Prefectural / Municipal Tax**: Additional local taxes apply

---

## Employment

- Social insurance required for full-time employees
- Labor Standards Act applies to all employees
- Use **freee HR** or **マネーフォワード** for payroll automation
