## Government Grants (補助金)

### ものづくり補助金 (Monodzukuri Subsidy)
- **For:** Manufacturing, technology development, service innovation
- **Amount:** Up to ¥12,500,000 (general), ¥25,000,000 (digital)
- **Apply:** [mirasapo-plus.smrj.go.jp](https://mirasapo-plus.smrj.go.jp/)

### IT導入補助金 (IT Introduction Subsidy)
- **For:** Digital tools and software adoption for SMEs
- **Amount:** Up to ¥4,500,000
- **Apply:** [it-hojo.jp](https://www.it-hojo.jp/)

### 小規模事業者持続化補助金 (Small Business Sustainability Subsidy)
- **For:** Marketing, DX, and business model expansion
- **Amount:** Up to ¥2,000,000
- **Apply:** [jizokuka-r3.smrj.go.jp](https://jizokuka-r3.smrj.go.jp/)

### 事業再構築補助金 (Business Restructuring Subsidy)
- **For:** Business model transformation and diversification
- **Amount:** Up to ¥150,000,000
- **Apply:** [jigyou-saikouchiku.go.jp](https://jigyou-saikouchiku.go.jp/)
