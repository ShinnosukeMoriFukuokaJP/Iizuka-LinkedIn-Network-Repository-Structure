## Why Build in Chikuho?

| Advantage | Details |
|-----------|---------|

| 🤝 **Community** | Tight-knit networks with high trust and referrals |

| 🎓 **University Talent** | Kyushu Institute of Technology pipeline |

## Growth Strategy: First Customers

**For B2B startups in Chikuho:**
1. Start with 1 design partner in your city (free or discounted)
2. Document the case study with measurable results
3. Post the case study on LinkedIn tagging local hashtags
4. Use the case study to close the next 3 customers
5. Join ILN LinkedIn Group to find referrals across all 11 cities

## Funding Landscape

### Government Programs
- **Fukuoka Prefecture Startup Support** — [pref.fukuoka.lg.jp](https://www.pref.fukuoka.lg.jp/)
- **METI Startup Programs** — [meti.go.jp](https://www.meti.go.jp/)

### Grants
- **Monodzukuri Hojyokin (ものづくり補助金)** — Manufacturing and tech
- **IT Dounyu Hojyokin (IT導入補助金)** — Digital transformation
