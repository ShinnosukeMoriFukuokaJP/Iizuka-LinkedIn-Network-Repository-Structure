## About the Mentor Program

The **Iizuka LinkedIn Network Mentor Program** connects early-stage founders with experienced professionals from the **Chikuho Region** and **Fukuoka Prefecture**.

All mentorship is **free** and volunteer-driven.

---

## Become a Mentor

We seek mentors with experience in:
- Building and scaling businesses in regional Japan
- AI and DX implementation
- Fundraising and investor relations
- Sales and B2B business development
- Manufacturing, healthcare, or agriculture industries
- LinkedIn and personal branding

**To apply as a mentor**, open a GitHub Issue with the label `mentor` or email [community@iizuka-linkedin-network.org](mailto:community@iizuka-linkedin-network.org)

---

## Find a Mentor

Post in the LinkedIn Group with:
- Your startup concept (one sentence)
- What kind of mentor you need
- Your location in the Chikuho Region
