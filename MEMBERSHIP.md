# Membership

**Iizuka LinkedIn Network** · Chikuho Region · Fukuoka Prefecture · Japan

Membership is **completely free**. There are no paywalls, no paid tiers, and no hidden fees.

---

## Who Should Join

- Professionals working in **Iizuka City**, **Tagawa City**, **Nogata City**, or anywhere in the **Chikuho Region**
- Students at universities and vocational schools in **Fukuoka Prefecture**
- Entrepreneurs and startup founders building in regional Japan
- Business owners looking to understand AI and Digital Transformation
- Career changers exploring opportunities in AI, DX, and tech
- Anyone passionate about **Regional Revitalization** in Japan
- International professionals with ties to or interest in **Fukuoka Prefecture**

---

## How to Join

### Step 1: GitHub
Star and watch this repository to stay updated on new resources and discussions.

### Step 2: LinkedIn Group
**[Join the LinkedIn Group →](https://www.linkedin.com/groups/)** *(link to be activated)*

Answer the three entry questions honestly — they help us understand your background and goals.

### Step 3: Newsletter
Subscribe to the monthly newsletter for curated AI, DX, and career content for Chikuho Region professionals.

### Step 4: Introduce Yourself
Post a brief introduction in the LinkedIn Group:
- Your name and location (city in Fukuoka Prefecture or elsewhere)
- Your professional background
- What you hope to get from and give to the community

---

## Member Expectations

We ask all members to:
- Adhere to our [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)
- Contribute at least occasionally — share a resource, comment on a post, or attend an event
- Keep promotional content to a minimum unless it genuinely serves the community
- Be welcoming to new members, especially those new to AI or LinkedIn

---

## Contributor Recognition

Active contributors receive:
- **Contributor badge** on their GitHub profile through this repository
- **Member spotlight** in the monthly newsletter
- **Featured profile** in the LinkedIn Group
- Early invitations to new events and programs
- Credit in the annual Chikuho Innovation Impact Report

## FAQ

**Q: Is there a membership fee?**
A: No. Membership is permanently free.

**Q: Can companies join as members?**
A: Yes. We welcome both individual professionals and organizational members.

**Q: I live outside the Chikuho Region. Can I join?**
A: Absolutely. The community is open to anyone, anywhere, who shares our values and interests.

**Q: How active do I need to be?**
A: There is no minimum activity requirement. Lurking is fine. Contributing is celebrated.
