## How to Join (4 Steps)

### Step 1: Star the Repository
⭐ [Star this repository](https://github.com/ShinnosukeMoriFukuokaJP/Iizuka-LinkedIn-Network-Repository-Structure) to stay updated.

### Step 2: Join the LinkedIn Group
**[→ Join Now](https://www.linkedin.com/groups/14722875)** *(link to be activated)*

Answer the three entry questions:
1. What city or region are you based in?
2. What is your professional background?
3. What do you hope to gain from or contribute to this community?

### Step 3: Introduce Yourself
Post in the LinkedIn Group:
- Your name and location
- Your professional background in one sentence
- One thing you want to learn and one thing you can teach

All members are equal. Contributors who are especially active receive additional recognition:

| Tier | How to Earn | Recognition |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| **Member** | Join LinkedIn Group | Welcome post |
| **Contributor** | Submit 1+ Pull Request | GitHub badge + newsletter mention |
| **Active Contributor** | 5+ contributions | Featured profile + LinkedIn spotlight |
| **Core Contributor** | Sustained, high-quality contributions | Co-maintainer invitation |

## Member Expectations

We ask members to:
- Follow the [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)
- Contribute at least occasionally — share a resource, comment, or attend an event
- Keep promotional content minimal and community-relevant
- Welcome new members warmly, especially those new to AI or LinkedIn

There is **no minimum activity requirement**. Lurking is fine. Contributing is celebrated.

## Growth Strategy

### First 10 Members — Week 1–2
1. Founder personal LinkedIn announcement with repository link
2. Direct message to 10 professionals in Iizuka City
3. Iizuka Chamber of Commerce outreach
4. Post in Fukuoka regional LinkedIn Groups
5. Kyushu Institute of Technology career office contact
6. Share in Chikuho LINE and community groups
7. #福岡 #筑豊 hashtag posts on Twitter/X
8. Personal network GitHub star campaign
9. 30-minute online intro session
10. Personal follow-up with every early joiner

### First 100 Members — Month 1–3
- LinkedIn content series 3x/week (12 weeks)
- First in-person event in Iizuka City
- Referral ask: every member invites 1 person
- Guest post in Fukuoka business media
- Newsletter launch to email list

### First 1,000 Members — Month 4–12
- Monthly events across Iizuka, Tagawa, Nogata
- Chikuho Innovation Summit (annual)
- Media coverage: regional and national press
- Corporate partnerships promoting membership
- University student program
- SEO-driven organic growth from repository

### First 10,000 Members — Year 2–4
- Multi-prefecture expansion
- National media features
- Government partnership programs
- Open-source replication kit for other regions

## FAQ

**Q: Is there a membership fee?**  
A: No. Membership is permanently free.

**Q: I'm not from the Chikuho Region. Can I join?**  
A: Yes. Anyone interested in regional Japan, AI, DX, or professional networking is welcome.

**Q: How active do I need to be?**  
A: No minimum. Even if you only use the resources occasionally, you're welcome.

**Q: Can I leave and rejoin?**  
A: Of course. The community is always open.

**Q: How do I become a maintainer?**  
A: Sustained, high-quality contribution over time. See [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md).
