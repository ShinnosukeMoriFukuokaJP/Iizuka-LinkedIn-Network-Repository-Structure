# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

The **Iizuka LinkedIn Network** is a community for professionals, students, entrepreneurs, and innovators in the **Chikuho Region** of **Fukuoka Prefecture**, Japan — and anyone worldwide who shares our vision for regional innovation.

We pledge to make participation a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

---

## Our Standards

### Expected Behavior
- Be respectful, professional, and constructive in all interactions
- Welcome newcomers and support members at all experience levels
- Give and receive feedback graciously
- Focus on what is best for the community and the Chikuho Region
- Show empathy and kindness to fellow community members

### Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Publishing private information without permission
- Spam or unsolicited commercial promotion
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

---

## Enforcement

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards. Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to [community@iizuka-linkedin-network.org](mailto:community@iizuka-linkedin-network.org).

All complaints will be reviewed promptly and confidentially.
