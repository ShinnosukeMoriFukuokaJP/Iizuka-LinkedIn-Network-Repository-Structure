# Code of Conduct

**Iizuka LinkedIn Network** · Chikuho Region · Fukuoka Prefecture · Japan

---

## Our Commitment

**Iizuka LinkedIn Network** is an open, professional community built for the **Chikuho Region** and everyone who supports regional innovation. We are committed to providing a harassment-free, inclusive, and constructive environment for all participants — regardless of age, background, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual orientation.

This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces: this GitHub repository, the LinkedIn Group, community events, and any official communications.

---

## Our Values

| Value | What It Means |
|-------|---------------|
| **Respect** | Treat every person with dignity, regardless of their background or experience level |
| **Inclusion** | Welcome newcomers, support beginners, and value diverse perspectives |
| **Professionalism** | Maintain the standards you would in any professional setting |
| **Constructiveness** | Offer feedback that helps, not hurts |
| **Integrity** | Be honest about your identity, affiliations, and intentions |
| **Community First** | Prioritize the good of the Chikuho Region community over individual gain |

---

## Expected Behavior

✅ Use welcoming and inclusive language  
✅ Respect differing viewpoints and experiences  
✅ Accept constructive criticism gracefully  
✅ Focus on what is best for the community  
✅ Show empathy toward fellow community members  
✅ Give credit when using others' work or ideas  
✅ Be patient with newcomers and non-technical contributors  

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

❌ Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind  
❌ Trolling, insulting, or demeaning comments  
❌ Personal or political attacks  
❌ Publishing others' private information without consent  
❌ Spam, unsolicited commercial promotion, or irrelevant content  
❌ Sustained disruption of community discussions  
❌ Any behavior considered inappropriate in a professional setting  

---

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. **GitHub**: Use the "Report" feature or email maintainers
2. **LinkedIn Group**: Use LinkedIn's report function
3. **Email**: [community@iizuka-linkedin-network.org](mailto:community@iizuka-linkedin-network.org)

All reports are reviewed confidentially. We take all reports seriously and will act appropriately.

---

## Enforcement

Community leaders may take the following actions in response to violations:

| Level | Behavior | Action |
|-------|----------|--------|
| 1 | Minor violation, first offense | Private warning |
| 2 | Repeated or significant violation | Temporary suspension |
| 3 | Severe or persistent violation | Permanent ban |

---

## Appeals

If you believe an enforcement action was taken in error, email [community@iizuka-linkedin-network.org](mailto:community@iizuka-linkedin-network.org) with "CoC Appeal" in the subject line.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all community spaces and when representing the community publicly (e.g., using official email addresses, posting via official social media accounts, or representing the community at events).
