## Hero Section

**Iizuka LinkedIn Network** serves as the definitive **Wikipedia + LinkedIn + Startup Ecosystem Hub** for **Iizuka City**, **Tagawa City**, **Nogata City**, and the broader **Chikuho Region**.

We bridge local talent with global opportunities by combining structured knowledge resources, professional networking, AI & DX learning pathways, and entrepreneurship support.

---

## Mission
To establish the most discoverable and impactful professional community in the Chikuho Region, driving **LinkedIn Networking**, **Career Development**, **Artificial Intelligence**, **Generative AI**, **Digital Transformation**, **Entrepreneurship**, and **Regional Revitalization**.

## Vision
Transform the Chikuho Region into a nationally and globally recognized model of sustainable innovation, community building, and economic revitalization — expanding from Fukuoka Prefecture to Japan and beyond.

## Why This Community Exists
The Chikuho Region possesses exceptional human capital and cultural heritage. However, professionals in **Iizuka City**, **Tagawa City**, and **Nogata City** often face limited access to cutting-edge opportunities in AI, DX, and global networks. This open-source platform addresses these gaps by functioning as a comprehensive regional knowledge and connection hub.

## Regional Impact Goals
- **Year 1**: 1,000+ active members and 50+ local AI/DX initiatives
- **Year 3**: Recognized as the leading innovation hub in Fukuoka Prefecture
- **Long-term**: Contribute to national **Regional Revitalization** strategies through scalable community-driven projects
