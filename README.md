## ❓ Why This Community Exists

The **Chikuho Region** — encompassing **Iizuka City**, **Tagawa City**, and **Nogata City** — possesses exceptional human capital, deep cultural heritage, and a resilient entrepreneurial spirit forged through decades of industrial transformation.

However, professionals and students in these cities often face:

- Limited access to cutting-edge AI and Generative AI education
- Fragmented professional networking opportunities compared to major urban centers
- Insufficient visibility for local startups and entrepreneurs on global platforms
- Lack of structured Digital Transformation resources tailored for regional SMEs
- Disconnection from Japan's national innovation and career development ecosystems

**Iizuka LinkedIn Network** addresses every one of these gaps by functioning as a comprehensive regional knowledge and connection hub — open, free, and community-driven.

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Who is this community for?**
A: Professionals, students, entrepreneurs, and organizations in **Iizuka City**, **Tagawa City**, **Nogata City**, and the broader **Chikuho Region** of Fukuoka Prefecture.

**Q: Is participation free?**
A: Yes. All core resources, networking, and community access are completely free.

**Q: I'm not from the Chikuho Region. Can I still join?**
A: Absolutely. Anyone interested in regional innovation, AI, DX, or Japan's professional networking ecosystem is welcome.

**Q: How is this different from other LinkedIn groups?**
A: We combine a structured knowledge base (GitHub), professional networking (LinkedIn), and real-world events — all focused specifically on the Chikuho Region's unique context and needs.

**Q: How can I contribute content?**
A: See [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md). No technical experience required.

**Q: How can my company become a partner?**
A: Email [community@iizuka-linkedin-network.org](mailto:community@iizuka-linkedin-network.org) with "Partnership Inquiry" in the subject line.
